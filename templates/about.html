About Cafe & Wifi

I created Cafe&Wifi as part of my portfolio to showcase both design and purpose. While working on this project, I was also immersing myself in the world of software testing — learning how to build, evaluate, and fine-tune digital experiences. Mapping out cozy cafés that offer great coffee and a comfortable space to get work done became the perfect way to combine my growing tech skills with a passion for discovering inspiring places that foster creativity and productivity.

These spaces are designed with laptop lovers, thinkers, dreamers, and caffeine enthusiasts in mind — wherever they may be. Whether you're deep in debugging code, writing a thesis, or sketching out your next big idea, cozy cafés around the world offer the ideal blend of inspiration, coffee, and connection, making them perfect for remote work, study, or a well-deserved break.

  • Specialty coffee blends
  • Free high-speed WiFi
  • Work-friendly tables
  • Cozy, welcoming atmosphere
